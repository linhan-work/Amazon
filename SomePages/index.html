Purple Lake Investment Ltd.
is an investment company based in Halifax, Nova Scotia.
To act as a bridge between Atlantic Canada and China
we help local business owners and Chinese investors
find more business opportunities.

Business Delegation Group Accommodation

Our company provides accommodation and transportation services to business delegation groups from China and other foreign countries. In addition to arrange hotel and book flight tickers, we make itinerary arrangements for business delegation group members to meet local law firms, banking advisors and accounting agency to understand basic rules and regulations of making investment in Atlantic Canada. We also schedule meeting for foreign investors to communicate with local industrial elites to understand in-depth knowledge of specific industry they are interested in. No matter foreign investors plan to start new business or acquire existing promising local firms, we will find the correct professional crew to provide them professional answers.

Business Consulting Service

Our research and consulting team conduct market research and feasibility analysis on specific industries based on foreign investor requirements. We also provide professional business consulting and strategic consulting service for foreign investors who plan to make investment in specific Atlantic firm or industry. In the past years we successfully assisted some Chinese investors find business opportunities in Atlantic Canada in the field of hotel and tourism, schooling, fishery, mining and agriculture, etc.

Fund Raising Consulting Service

With years of working experience in Asian investment banking industry, our company owner established sold networking in Chinese venture capital and private equity industry. Following the mushroom of international trade and industrial corporation between Canada and China, more small and medium business owners in Atlantic areas are looking for oversea capital to help them expand business to oversea market. Also, Chinese investors desire to distribute their fortune to safe and friendly developed countries such as Canada. Our company successfully helped some local fisherman obtain foreign capital to sell various fishery products with higher price to Chinese market. We also helped a local medical care service provider receive venture capital investment from China. This reciprocal consulting service will be our core business in the future years.

Foreign Students Study Tour Program

Study Tours are short-term academic programs that allow foreign students to complete an intensive course in overseas. They may include multiple stops at multiple destinations or be a more immersive experience at just one. Study Tours are usually 1-3 weeks in length and take place within semester breaks. As long-term partner with some of the best private and public high schools in China, our company is running a few study tour programs with high schools in British Columbia now. Next year we plan to launch new programs with Atlantic partners.
